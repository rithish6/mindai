# EduMind AI - Presentation Content

## Slide 1: Title

EduMind AI - Intelligent AI-Powered Learning Management Platform

Presented by: Rithish

## Slide 2: Introduction

EduMind AI is an AI-powered platform that converts educational content into personalized learning resources such as notes, summaries, flashcards, quizzes, mind maps, podcasts, and tutor answers.

## Slide 3: Problem Statement

Students spend too much time organizing materials, summarizing chapters, preparing quizzes, revising lectures, and searching for answers. Existing learning platforms store content but do not deeply personalize learning from uploaded materials.

## Slide 4: Proposed Solution

EduMind AI allows students to upload files and links, then uses AI to generate interactive resources. The AI Tutor answers questions from uploaded materials using Retrieval-Augmented Generation.

## Slide 5: Key Features

- Document upload and processing
- AI notes and summaries
- Flashcards and quizzes
- RAG-based AI Tutor
- Mind maps
- AI podcast generation
- Lecture transcription
- Image-based doubt solver
- Study planner
- Progress analytics
- Multilingual translation

## Slide 6: System Architecture

Frontend: Next.js and TypeScript

Backend: FastAPI

Database: PostgreSQL and Redis

AI Layer: LLM, embeddings, RAG, Whisper, OCR

Storage: AWS S3 or Cloudinary

Deployment: Vercel and Railway or Render

## Slide 7: RAG-Based AI Tutor

The AI Tutor retrieves relevant chunks from uploaded study materials and uses them as context for the language model. This improves accuracy and keeps answers grounded in the user's content.
